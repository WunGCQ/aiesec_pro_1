International internship

INTERNATIONAL INTERNSHIP

Global Talents is a international internship program for young people who want to gain professional experience. Internship can be held in one of 4 subprograms: Business, Marketing, Education and IT in more than 100 countries around the world.

http://globaltalents.pl/
International volunteer

INTERNATIONAL VOLUNTEER

Global Citizen an international exchange program for young people interested in personal development, exploration and sharing of your culture. Taking part in our program you can visit most exotic parts of the World, experience the adventure of your life and help other people.

http://globalcitizen.pl/
Language courses

LANGUAGE COURSES

AIESEC University is the program of innovative language courses provided by young native speakers from all of the world. During classes we are focused on attractive and practical learning of language. Te idea of AIESEC University is Learn By Play that's why our courses are so unique and gives satisfaction for students and teachers.

http://learnbyplay.pl/
Poland Youth to Business Forum

POLAND YOUTH TO BUSINESS FORUM

Youth to Business Forum  is an initiative powered by AIESEC and aims to connect young leaders and businesses around relevant topics in the country to generate new, actionable ideas and thoughts about the future.

http://y2bpoland.pl/

We believe in the power of good leadership. That's why in AIESEC we focus on shaping future leaders today who will be responsible for deciding how the world is lead tomorrow. Through our global leadership develop programmes, we aim to create remarkable impact on the lives of a million young people in over 120 countries and territories within the next 3 years. This is our contribution to building a better future for all of us.

Career Days

CAREER DAYS

Carrier Days are the biggest job and internship fairs in Poland. Held for more than 20 years in the academic cities. Each year over 60,000 students andabout 250 companies visits our event.

http://dnikariery.pl/
